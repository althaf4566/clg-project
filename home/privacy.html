Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how JobRecko (referred to as we,us,our) collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our job recommendation services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you directly provide to us, such as your profile details (including OS\_percent, Coding\_Rating, etc.), career goals, and job preferences. This information is essential for generating personalized job recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize job recommendations based on your profile data.
  • To improve and analyze our services, including the performance of our recommendation algorithms.
  • To communicate with you regarding service updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized or aggregated data for research or business analysis. We will only share identifiable information (like your resume or contact details) with third-party employers with your explicit consent when you choose to apply for a job.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including data encryption and access controls, to protect your information from unauthorized access and disclosure. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can review and update your profile information at any time through the Profile page. You also have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data.

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@jobrecko.com.
